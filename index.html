The Crystals Collector Game

Instructions:


You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your score, so pay attention!

Match your total score to the random number and you win! Go over, and you lose! Each time the game resets, the values of each crystal will change!!
